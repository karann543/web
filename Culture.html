Festivals

india celebrates a variety of festivals that reflect its rich cultural heritage and religious diversity

  • Diwali:Festivals of light,symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.
  • Holi:Festivals of colors,celebrating love and the aarival of spring.
  • Eid:Festival of Harmony,marked by prayers,feasts and charity.
  • ChristmasFestival of joy,celebrating the birth of jesus christ.

  • LehengaA festival outfit for woman,especially in wedding.
  • Dhoti:Traditional men's wear in southern and eastern india
